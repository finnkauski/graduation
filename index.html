PSA: Imposter syndrome

Summary of Caitlin Hudon's Blog

Data Science‚Ñ¢, a fertile ground for imposter syndrome

  • Data Science‚Ñ¢ is a new field

  • Data Science‚Ñ¢ is a combination of other fields

  • Data Science‚Ñ¢ is constantly expanding with new tech

We don't really know what is yet. Sort of.

Its perfectly normal to have gaps given that you can't be all at once

New tech comes about and you feel pressured to stay on top of things.

How to deal with it?

  • We are ALL learning as we go! It's exciting! Acknowledge that!

  • Accept that you won't be able to learn EVERY area of Data Science‚Ñ¢

  • Nobody will!

  • I was much more on the coding side, but not great at stats

From keyboard shortcuts to a new programming language!

How to deal with it?

  • You will know things nobody else does.

  • Get comfortable with "I don't know"

  • Don't `fake it 'til you make it`. Its stressful and harmful for development.

  • Don't compare yourself too much to others, compare yourself to who you were yesterday.
